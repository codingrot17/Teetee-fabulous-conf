About Us

We are TEETEE FABULOUS

Our Mission

TEETEE FABULOUS inspires and innovates with a unique & wide range of Cakes. This allows us to accommodate consumer wishes and expectations through high-quality products with authentic flavours, appearances and taste sensations. Challenging and stimulating the product category on a daily basis creates growth for our partners.

Our Vision

To build a leading international company passionate about Cakes with the drive to grow and develop the confectionery market, through our longstanding expertise (historical, traditional), innovation and empowered talent.

Our Values

  • Entrepreneurship – We are proactive and seek out opportunities for innovation
  • No-nonsense – We work efficiently thanks to our open, direct communication
  • Respect – We respect one another, our products and the planet
  • Ownership – We don't shirk our responsibilities and look after the organization's interests
  • Passion – We are enthusiastic, proud and continuously strive for top quality
